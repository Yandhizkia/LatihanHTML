About Game

Need for Speed Heat is a 2019 open-world racing game set in Palm City.

During the day, players can participate in a racing event called the Speedhunter Showdown. Compete and earn money for every win. Use the rewards to tweak your car performance and visual custom parts. Spend as much time as you like. When you're ready, switch it to night. You can challenge yourself by competing in street races to get reputation and get access for better performance parts. This game also offers a great storyline. So what do you waiting for? Download Need for Speed Heat now!
